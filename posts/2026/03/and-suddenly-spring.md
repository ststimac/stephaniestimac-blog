And suddenly it happens one day in March. 
The sun beams down in a rare cloudless sky. 
It might be cold as the wind gently blows but
The Sun. The Sun beams down and it promises 
Long nights on the horizon. 
It promises miserable humid days 
And lovely warm days.
Harsh mid-day light.
Dreamy golden evenings.
It comes out to announce Winter's end before
It once again disappears behind England's cloudy skies.
And Suddenly Spring is here and my soul is warmed once again.
